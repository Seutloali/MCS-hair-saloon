About

MCS hair studio is a braids inclined service hair salon located in Ha-Leqele, Maseru, Lesotho. Our services includes hair wash, braids, butterfly locks, with passion to expand in hair cut and other hairstyles in the foreseeable future. We are committed to providing our clients with highest quality services and products. We use only the best products, skills and tools available to ensure that our clients leave with our salon looking and feeling their best. Our dedicated work has positioned us as one of the best braiding hair salon. We adapt to both previous and modern culture, formal or informal, which makes us a perfect place to be, for any personal preferences a client might have. We take professionalism and attention to details very seriously, therefore, if you want to wear a magical crown, do stop.
